Since Israel was founded in 1948, three quarters of a million Palestinians have been forced from their homes.

In most cases, Israel simply seized their land.

But more recently, Israel has displaced Palestinians in another way: through deportation…

Exiling them from Israel, the occupied West Bank and Gaza.

It’s illegal under international law, but Israeli officials say it’s their right to protect national security.

Some deportees admit they’ve committed acts of violence -- in the name of resisting the occupation….

Like Ahlam al Tamimi who helped in a suicide attack...

But others, like Mustafa Hamayel were deported, for a minor offense.

While Palestinians like Mamdouh Al Wardian agreed to be deported, under pressure from Israel.
