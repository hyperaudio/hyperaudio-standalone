The reconstruction scenes in this documentary portray the daily life of Palestinian prisoners held in Israeli jails. Freed prisoner Ayman Al Sharawna has volunteered to act in this documentary to reflect the reality of detention and hunger strike which he himself lived inside Israeli jails.

The reconstruction scenes were filmed in Al Saraya prison which had been used by the Israeli authorities for 27 years. Several hunger strikes took place in Al Saraya prison.
