Eras

Browse video-content by key periods

  1. Era I 1799 – 1947 ?
    This is Era 1 - (the story of transferring Palestine into a Jewish state started politically in 1799 when Napoleon (France) called for that). This era ends with the UN officially adopting a resolution of partition of Palestine into an Arab and Jewish state (in 29 Nov 1947)
  2. Era II 1948 – 1949 ?
    This is Al Nakba Climax (which means the massive ethnic cleansing and expulsion of over 800 000 Palestinians from their homes, erasing Palestine from the world map and transferring it into Israel, a country recognized by the UN, the international community and dealing with it in political arena).
  3. Era III 1950 – 1967 ?
    This is Era 3 where Israel is established and continued into slow ethnic cleansing and expulsion and military rule of over 150 000 Palestinians who remain inside Israel and were forced to hold Israeli passports and forced to live in Palestinians ghettos inside Israel. And making sure not to allow Palestinians refugees the right to return.
  4. Era IV 1968 – 1992 ?
    This is Era 4 where Israel occupied the rest of history Palestine (known as the West Bank and Gaza Strip) in addition to the Golan Heights and Egyptian Sinai. Causing more than 300 000 out of their homes, some of them refugees for the 2nd time in their lives. International community did not recognize Israeli settlements (colonies) inside the occupied territories and still regard them according to international law illegal. In this era the apartheid system was created and the Palestinians ghettos inside the occupied territories.
  5. Era V 1993 – 2014 ?
    This is Era 5 – for the first time Israel recognized the PLO (Palestinians Liberation Movement) and sign with it a peace accord, and the world accepted Israel as a peace lover country. Yet, on the ground occupation continues, ethnic cleansing and expulsion continues and more and more Palestinians both inside Israel and in the occupied territories are subject to discrimination, humiliation and living in ghettos that were bombed by F-16 for the first time in 2001, a big blockage on Gaza in 2005 and then with massive war with thousands of causalities starting in 2008 on Gaza.
