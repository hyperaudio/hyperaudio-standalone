Palestine Remix

The story of Palestine told like never before. Explore, browse and search through 20+ hours of top quality full-lenght documentaries reviving dialogue on the Palestine Issue in a new highly interactive way.

17 Films

17 Titles in four different languages on the story of Palestine

In Palestine, carrying a camera is dangerous. In Palestine, being a filmmaker is extremely difficult. Therefore it was not easy to produce and commission these films. Each film is a documentary that is full of drama, the drama of ethnic cleansing and the resistance of ethnic cleansing. These stories never end. As you are reading these lines, the Palestinians are living under occupation, siege and/or discrimination, waiting for justice and peace to come their way.
